About Us

Buddy's Bagels got its start in May 2011 by Buddy LaCostanza and his wife Betty. They raised their three kids to be bagel lovers, just like them. The LaCostanza's take great pleasure in sharing their passion of bagels with their community every day. Buddy's Bagels are made fresh every morning, and the leftovers are donated to the local food bank!
